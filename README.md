# Excel Projects

This folder contains three Excel workbooks. Each project shows a different use of Excel, including business tracking, data cleaning, and social research analysis.

## Card.xlsx

This Excel workbook was created in the summer of 2020 during the first few months of starting a small business selling playing cards on eBay.

My brother and I were given stock for free by a family member who previously ran the business. Although this version is from the early stage of the business, the spreadsheet was used to help track and organise stock, sales, and business information as the project developed.

## The Process of Table Cleaning.xlsx

This is a simple Excel project showing how basic formulas can be used to tidy, clean, and organise messy data.

The workbook demonstrates how Excel can be used to improve the structure and readability of raw data, making it easier to analyse and present.

## Survey and Interview Analysis.xlsx

This workbook was created for a university group project involving social research analysis.

It was used to organise and summarise survey and interview data, helping the group and lecturers understand what the data showed and how it related to the wider research project.
